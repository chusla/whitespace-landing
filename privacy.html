12. GOOGLE CALENDAR INTEGRATION

12.1 Opt-In Connection

Whitespace offers an optional Google Calendar integration that allows you to receive personalized meditation suggestions based on your schedule. This integration is entirely opt-in — your Google Calendar is never accessed unless you explicitly connect it within the app.

12.2 Data We Access and Collect

When you connect your Google Calendar, we use the Google Calendar API with the calendar.events.readonly scope. We collect and store the following information from your calendar:

  • Event times (start and end)
  • Event titles
  • Event durations

We do not access or store attendee lists, event descriptions, attachments, or any other calendar metadata beyond what is listed above.

12.3 How We Use This Data

Your calendar data is used solely to power personalized meditation suggestions — for example, suggesting a calming session before a busy block of meetings. Specifically, your calendar data is:

  • Never used for advertising or marketing purposes
  • Never sold to or shared with third parties
  • Never used to train general-purpose AI or machine learning models

12.4 Data Security

Calendar data is securely transmitted using TLS/SSL encryption and stored encrypted at rest on our servers. Access to stored calendar data is restricted to automated systems that generate your personalized suggestions.

12.5 Human Access

No Whitespace employee or contractor will read your calendar data unless:

  • You have given explicit consent (e.g., for a support request)
  • It is necessary for security purposes or to investigate abuse
  • It is required by applicable law

12.6 Disconnecting and Data Deletion

You can disconnect your Google Calendar at any time from within the app. Upon disconnection:

  • We immediately stop accessing your Google Calendar
  • You may request deletion of all stored calendar data from within the app
  • Deleted calendar data is permanently removed from our servers within 30 days

If you delete your Whitespace account, all stored calendar data is also deleted as described in Section 4.5.

12.7 Google API Services User Data Policy Compliance

Our use of Google Calendar data complies with Google's API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Whitespace's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.
